PROJECT OVERVIEW

The Smart Modular Canopy System is a weather-responsive outdoor
shelter designed to enhance usability and comfort in campus spaces.
Using automation and modular design principles, the system integrates
environmental sensors to detect rain and temperature changes,
allowing automatic adjustments without manual intervention.
Inspired by biomimicry and cybernetic theory, its origami-based
structure ensures flexibility while optimizing space. Evaluated at
Cebu Technological University, the system received a high acceptability
rating for its features, performance, and aesthetics, proving its
effectiveness in adapting to environmental conditions. Future refinements
will focus on improving speed, noise reduction, and seamless integration
to further enhance its functionality.
